inline-block

inline-block is a friendly Slack-powered community consisting of frontend and backend developers, UX and UI designers, digital marketers, and more.

While many online communities are focused around specific roles or tools, what makes inline-block unique is the diverse mix of experience and skills. It's a great place to learn from others and ask questions or to share your work and get feedback.

There's plenty of web talk, of course. We love code and design. But there's also channels for books, business and freelancing, productivity, routines & habits, photography, and more.

We're a friendly, welcoming group. Whether you're a freelancer, agency owner, or in a full-time position, we think you'll get a lot from our community.

Want in? Request an invite.

When you arrive:

  • Go and introduce yourself in #intro. Say as much or as little as you like.
  • Explore the channels.
  • Read the code of conduct.
